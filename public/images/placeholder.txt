Please export the following images from Figma and place them in this folder:

2. phone-mockup-2.png - The single phone mockup in the middle section

To export from Figma:
1. Open the Figma file
2. Select each image/frame
3. Click "Export" in the right panel
4. Choose PNG format
5. Click "Export" and save to this folder
